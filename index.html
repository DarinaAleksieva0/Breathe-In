Our Yoga Programs

Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga focuses on the correct alignment of each of the joint areas for maximum stability, so that a pose can be held.

Difficulty level: Sustainable for all levels.

Iyengar Yoga

Iyengar Yoga focuses on alignment as well as detailed and precise movements. In an Iyengar class, students perform a variety of postures while controlling the breath..

Difficulty level: Sustainable for all levels.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga shtanga starts with five sun salutation A's and five sun salutation B's and then moves into a series of standing and floor postures.

Difficulty level: Sustainable for advanced level.
